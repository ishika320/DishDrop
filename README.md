# 🍽️ DishDrop

DishDrop is a **food reel sharing platform** where food partners can upload short food videos and users can discover delicious dishes visually, similar to Instagram Reels but focused on food.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 👤 User
- Browse food reels
- Like food videos
- Comment on reels
- Save favorite dishes
- Visit food partner stores

### 🧑‍🍳 Food Partner
- Register as a food partner
- Upload food reels
- Manage profile and listings
- Showcase food visually
